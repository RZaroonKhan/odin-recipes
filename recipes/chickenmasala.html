Chicken Masala

Description

It is a chicken curry with a thick sauce/gravy typically eaten with rice or chappati. It originates from South Asia and features spices from the Indian subcontinent.

It is not a vegetarian dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Garam Masala
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yoghurt

Instructions

  1. Finely dice onions
  2. Caramelise onions and throw in spices
  3. Cut tomatoes in half and place them face down until they soften
  4. Mash tomatoes and onions together and put in yoghurt
  5. Add chicken breast and cook on low heat until chicken is fully cooked and the water has evaporated
